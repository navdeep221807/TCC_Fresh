Molecular Mayhem - YanTra'25

Date: YANTRA–25

The Catalyst Club brought the fun and frenzy of chemistry to YanTra'25 with "Molecular Mayhem"! Twelve teams dove into a whirlwind of science, starting with an inspiring guest talk on marine tech. The competition kicked off with a lively quiz, then ramped up to a buzzer round where speed and smarts were key. The final memory game had everyone on their toes, as teams raced to remember and recall chemistry items in the right order. The event wrapped up with big smiles and a group photo to capture the awesome energy of the day!
